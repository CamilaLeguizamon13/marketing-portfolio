# UUNN Strategic Marketing Campaign

## Overview
The UUNN strategic marketing campaign was launched in September 2024 and ran until December 2024. The campaign aimed to enhance brand awareness, drive customer engagement, and increase sales conversions.

## Objectives
- Increase brand awareness among target demographics by 30%.
- Achieve a 20% growth in customer engagement on digital platforms.
- Generate a 15% increase in sales conversions during the campaign period.

## Metrics
- Website traffic analytics before and after the campaign.
- Social media engagement rates (likes, shares, comments) during the campaign.
- Conversion rates from targeted ad campaigns.

- Digital Marketing Strategy
- Data Analysis & Reporting
- Content Creation
- Social Media Management
- SEO & SEM Strategies
